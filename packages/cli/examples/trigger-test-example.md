concepts:

  - ignores-irrelevant

---

# Positive Triggers

These queries should activate the skill:

- "Help me set up a development environment"
- "Can you configure my project?"
- "I need assistance with installation"

# Negative Triggers

These queries should NOT activate the skill:

- "What's the weather today?"
- "Tell me a joke"
- "How do I cook pasta?"
